# Changelog - May 23, 2026

## Objective: Recover Search Visibility and Pass Link Equity to Soil Reports

Following a Google Search Console (GSC) audit that revealed a 71% drop in overall impressions and the de-indexing of programmatic `/learn` soil report pages, we implemented a search recovery protocol.

### 1. Hybrid Title Tag Strategy
To reclaim exact-match rankings while retaining high-CTR urgency:
*   **Service Pages (`app/services/foundation-repair/[city]/page.tsx`):** Changed metadata title from `"Stop Foundation Damage in [City]: Forensic Evaluation & Risk Registry"` to `"Stop Foundation Damage in [City]: Foundation Distress Identification & Free Evaluation"`. This re-injects the high-ranking `"Foundation Distress Identification"` exact keyword string.
*   **Soil Reports (`app/learn/[slug]/page.tsx`):** Changed metadata title from `"[City] Foundation Warning: [Soil Unit] Soil Risk Analysis"` to `"[City] Foundation Settling Warning: [Soil Unit] Soil Risk Analysis"`. This targets the high-intent `"foundation settling"` search queries that had regressed.

### 2. Internal Linking & PageRank Recovery
To address the de-indexation of `/learn` pages:
*   **Dynamic Linking Widget:** Appended a prominent geological library card to the bottom of all dynamic city service pages. This links directly to their corresponding `/learn/[slug]-soil-analysis` reports. 
*   **Objective:** Passes strong PageRank / link equity from dynamic city service pages (which Google continues to crawl) directly to the soil reports, forcing Googlebot to discover and re-index the programmatic soil reports.

### 3. Compilation Status
*   Ran type checking (`npx tsc --noEmit`) with **0 errors**.
