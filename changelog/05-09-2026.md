# Changelog - May 09, 2026

## Objective: Solve the "Zero-Click Authority Trap"
Following a GSC API performance audit, it was discovered that while the site achieved top-3 rankings for forensic terms (e.g., "distress identification"), the CTR remained low due to an overly institutional tone. This update pivots the branding toward **Urgent Solutions**.

### 1. GSC Performance Audit (USA Only)
*   **Ranking Win:** Achieved Position **1.68** for `foundation distress identification in lewisville, tx` (Up from 9.8).
*   **Trend Identified:** "Evaluation" queries are outperforming "Inspection" queries by 6x in rank position.
*   **Critical Issue:** Identified a "Zero-Click" pattern where the site acted as a reference registry but failed to capture the lead.

### 2. CTR Metadata Overhaul
*   **Global Layout:** Updated metadata to include ⚠️ warnings and explicit "Forensic Evaluation" offers.
*   **Service Pages:** Redesigned Title Tags to lead with **"Stop Foundation Damage"** instead of just "Identification."
*   **Soil Reports (Learn):** Transitioned from "Soil Analysis" to **"Foundation Warning"** to increase urgency for homeowners in high-PI zones.

### 3. UI/UX "Solution" Pivot
*   **Navbar CTA:** Updated primary action from "Analyze Risk" to **"Get Forensic Eval"** to emphasize the human/professional service over the data tool.
*   **Homepage Hero:** Refined copy to emphasize **"Stopping Structural Damage"** as the primary outcome of the risk evaluation.

### 4. Technical Consolidation
*   **Redirect Monitoring:** Confirmed that Google is still serving `non-www` versions in some regions; reinforced canonical strategy in page templates.

**Status:** High-Intent Conversion Ready. Monitoring GSC for CTR improvement over the next 72 hours.
